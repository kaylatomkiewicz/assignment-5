Welcome

Come on down to the Ridgewater Community Pool and have a fun day with the kids or with your friends. All ages are welcome! Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult 18 years of age or older. Change rooms, lockers, and showers are provided for men, women, and families. Bring your own lock, or rent one for $1.00. We can't wait to see you!

We are located at 3556 Trout Rd, Ridgewater, Ontario. You can contact us at (416) 555-3298.
